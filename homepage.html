My Projects

Generic Gardens

Led a small group of developers to create a shopping website where customers can buy local farm fresh produce and where farmers can market their produce to their community. I worked on both the front end and back end of the utilizing react, mongoDB, node.js, etc. Customers can buy trending and fresh produce while supporting thier local farms. We believe that farm to table isn't a trend. It's the way food is suppose to be.

Afterschool

Head programmer for the VR horror puzzle game Afterschool. The objective of this game is to solve a series of puzzle scattered around the map while also trying to avoid the monster that lurks in the hallways. Each puzzle will grant the player a clue to the next puzzle and if you get caught, it's detention for you. I was in charge for programming most of the puzzles, interactables, and modeling mutiple assets in the scene.

Tunnel Vision

Developed game for the Games4Change event at my high school. This was my first experience with game development and I was able to program everything in the Unity game engine. The Games4Change this year was able the importance of family during difficult times, therefore this game's objective was to find all your family memebers before the next storm approaches. This project was built for windows, mac, and WebGL platforms.

Pizza Swamp

A VR arcade game where the player can have the authentic arcade experience at home! The bright vibrant lights illuminating the dark room gives a cozy atmosphere for the player to enjoy. The infinite coin machine allows players to play as longs as they want and the VCR player lets users change the music to thier liking. This game is also networked with Photon so players can enjoy the experience with all thier friends.

Peach Airlines

Worked with a small team to develop a fictional airline website for the 2020 FBLA website competition. The airline serves customers in states surrounding georgia and gives them a comfortable and affordable experience. The airline features a backend database system using Wix to store customer information securly. As the lead designer, I was responsible for making the website user freindly and aesthetically pleasing.
