Genetic Cars

Performed a genetic evolution experiment with cars in the unity game engine. Cars would consist of multiple bases like truck, suv, sedan, etc with continious properties such as wheel size, suspension travel, engine horsepower, and more. The intial population of cars randomly generated these genes are were tasks to travel a trecherous terrain. The farther an individual made it, the more liely thier genes would be passed on to the next generation. In the end, the most capable offroad vehicle strived.

Infect, Detect, Protect

Utilizing Unity's ML agents to train them under reinforcement learning and imitation learning to attack dangerous cancer while while avoiding healthy cells. I programmed "white knights" that act similar to white blood cells to identify cancerous cells based on thier behaviors and genetic makeup. The combination of RL and imitation learning proved to reinforce the agent's learning to a point where it can confidently attack cancerous cells swiftly before they multiply.

Fuzzy Racing

Developed a 3D racing game in Unity that features an AI-controlled car powered by fuzzy logic. Instead of relying on fixed rules or traditional pathfinding, the AI adapts its steering, acceleration, and braking based on fuzzy logic inference, allowing for more human-like and dynamic driving behavior. The system evaluates inputs such as speed, distance from track boundaries, and angle of turns to make real-time decisions, resulting in smoother and more realistic gameplay.
